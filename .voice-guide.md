## ADHD-Specific Guidelines

Kai has ADHD. These aren't just nice-to-haves â€” they're functional requirements.

1. **One thing at a time.** Never show two calls-to-action. "Check Answers" OR "Show Answer Key" â€” not both at once until answers are submitted.
2. **Visual hierarchy is focus management.** The question Kai is working on should be visually dominant. Everything else recedes.
3. **Progress is visible.** "Question 7 of 20" always shown. Kai needs to know where he is in the test.
4. **No walls of text.** Hints are one sentence. Solutions are numbered steps. Feedback is one line.
5. **Timer is motivating, not stressful.** Show time remaining, not time elapsed. Remaining time creates gentle urgency. Elapsed time creates anxiety.
6. **Celebrate completion, not just scores.** "Done. 20/20 answered." matters even if the score isn't perfect. Finishing is an achievement when focus is hard.
