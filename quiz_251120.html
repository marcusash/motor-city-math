Question 1
Sarah subscribes to a cloud storage service. She starts the year with 150 GB of available storage. Each month, her photos and videos automatically upload and use 8.5 GB of her storage space.

a) Write a function S(m) for the storage space S remaining (in GB) after m months.

b) Draw a graph of the remaining storage as a function of months.

c) Find S(7) and explain what it means in the context of the problem.

d) Find the intercepts and explain what they mean in the context of the problem.

e) What is an appropriate domain for your function? Explain why you chose it.

f) What does the slope mean in the context of the problem?

Question 2

c) Keeping in mind that the function is exponential, by what factor did the car's value change each year? Question 4
