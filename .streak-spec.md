When an incorrect answer breaks the streak:

## Behavior

1. **Starts at 0 (hidden).** First correct answer shows "ğŸ”¥ 1 in a row" with fade-in.
2. **Increments on correct.** Each correct answer bumps the number. Pulse animation on 3, 5, 10.
3. **Resets on incorrect.** Fades out. Counter goes to 0.
4. **Persists during test.** The streak is per-session only. Not saved to localStorage.
5. **Post-submission.** Streak counter hides. Score card takes over.
6. **Best streak.** Track highest streak during the test. Show in score card: "Best streak: 7 ğŸ”¥"
